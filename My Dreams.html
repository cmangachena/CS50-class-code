I grew up seeing a lot of injustice around me, how rich people made poor people even poorer, how the tables had never turned for generations. I lost two people dear to me because of lack of adequate facilities in hospitals. People responsible had collected, pocketed and misused funds. Lots of corruption. Therefore, I am quite passionate about public health and the environment. This is a common sight in many places all over the world and it breaks my heart:

I love order and I want to be among those that will be responsible for bringing the change to underdeveloped countries. I dream of bringing recycling and environmental awareness to Africa on a very high level. Proper disposal of waste and waste management, is my life-long dream to help all of Africa and beyond look as clean as Rwanda.." .
