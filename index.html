Welcome to Chenai"s homepage

all things BEADS...and me!

This is the space where you access the peep-hole to my soul...! I am sharing with you my uncommon hobby that was birthed from my strong, passionate desire to keep the environment clean! Believe me, it is as beautiful as it is weird...do not freak out. Drumroll please....

Hang on for just a bit though...

I would like you to know a few things about me... I am Class of 2022 student at Harvard College, from Zimbabwe and my love for bead-making developed in my last few years of high school. A close acquintance of mine called Rebekah approached me and a group of friends a while ago during my preparation for college and she wanted us to help her fund-raise for her non-profit organization EducationMatters, and so she taught me how to make the beads. I learned it pretty quickly and easeily and I grew to like it.

This may seem uninteresting, but believe me, you are at risk of being contaminated with a high-dose of awesomenss!! Proceed at your own risk! BEAD-MAKING is where all the fun is dished out!
